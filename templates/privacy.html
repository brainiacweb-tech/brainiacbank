Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At BrainiacBank, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our digital banking platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when opening an account, including your full name, email address, phone number starting with +233, and physical address for card orders. We also collect verification documents (KYC) to comply with regulatory standards.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is utilized solely to provide secure financial services, process transactions, prevent fraudulent actions, issue virtual and physical debit cards, and send automated notifications about your deposits and withdrawals.

3. Information Security

We employ advanced 256-bit encryption protocols, secure session management, and robust database constraints to protect your funds and personal information. We never sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.
